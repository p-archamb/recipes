### Margarita classic:tequila

Ingredients | Amount | Unit
Tequila blanco | 1.5 | oz
Cointreau | .5 | oz
Fresh lime juice | .75 | oz
Salt | for rim |
Lime wheel | garnish |

1. Run a lime wedge around the rim of a margarita glass and dip in salt.
2. Add tequila, cointreau, and lime juice to a shaker with ice.
3. Shake.
4. Strain into the prepared glass over fresh ice.
5. Garnish with a lime wheel.

### Clover Club classic:gin

Ingredients | Amount | Unit
Gin  | 1.5 | oz
Dry Vermouth | .5 | oz
Fresh lemon juice | .5 | oz
Raspberry Syrup | .5 | oz
Egg White | .25 | oz
Raspberry | garnish |

1. Make raspberry syrup
    300g of fresh raspberries
    300g of cane sugar
    Smash raspberries into a juicy pulp, top and fold in sugar. 
    Let rest in a container in the fridge for up to 72 hours, stirring and agitating daily to fully incorporate the sugar. 
    Strain out solids, store in glass container for up to 2 weeks.
2. Add all ingredients without ice, shake. Be careful, egg whites add pressure and it often explodes
3. Add ice, and shake again until cold
4. Double strain into coupe glass
5. Garnish with a raspberry
